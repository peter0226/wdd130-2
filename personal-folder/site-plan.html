Overview

Purpose

The website aims to promote an event at the Palacio de Bellas Artes in Mexico City that will take place on September 15th, 16th and 17th, 2023 to commemorate the anniversary of the Mexican folkloric ballet. In addition, people can reserve or buy their tickets from the site.

Audience

The target audience is adults between the ages of 25-60 mainly with income with a taste for art or culture who access from any device.

Content

Home page

Join us to celebrate the anniversary of the Mexican Folkloric Ballet in one of Mexico's most iconic cultural institutions! The event will take place at the Palacio de Bellas Artes in Mexico City on September 15th, 16th, and 17th, 2023. Don't miss this unique opportunity to experience Mexico's rich cultural heritage!

The Folkloric Ballet is one of Mexico's most authentic and vibrant cultural expressions. In this special event, the artists will dance to the rhythm of traditional music, taking the audience through different regions of Mexico and their unique cultures. With over 50 years of history, the Mexican Folkloric Ballet is recognized worldwide for its artistic excellence and for being a cultural ambassador of Mexico to the world.

Reserve your tickets now and join the celebration of the Mexican Folkloric Ballet at the Palacio de Bellas Artes in September 2023!
